Press Release — Expansion Announcement

ZelDigital Eyes 2027 Expansion Into AI Automation, Corporate Training, and Global Markets

Pending Business Financing Approval — Company positions for next growth phase across the Philippines, the United States, and international markets

Philippines / May 24, 2026

Zel Digital Marketing Services OPC (doing business as ZelDigital), a performance-driven digital marketing company specializing in demand capture and sales growth, today announced its 2027 expansion plans contingent upon the approval of a business financing application filed with the Small Business Corporation (SB Corp). If approved, the funding will accelerate the company's growth into four new service areas and multiple international markets.

The planned expansion would transition ZelDigital from a focused digital marketing operation into a full-spectrum business growth platform — extending its core demand capture capabilities into AI automation, corporate training, enterprise paid advertising systems, and IT infrastructure solutions for corporate clients.

"We have spent years helping businesses turn the right traffic into real sales. The next phase is building the systems and teams that let us do that at a much bigger scale — across more markets, more industries, and more channels."
Hanzel Lacida
Chief Executive Officer, ZelDigital

"Financing approval is the trigger. The plan is already built."

What the Expansion Covers

Should financing be approved, ZelDigital intends to deploy capital across four integrated service lines:

01
Service Line
AI Business Automation

AI-driven systems that streamline client operations, reduce manual processes, and sharpen decision-making across corporate functions.

02
Service Line
Corporate Training Programs

Structured learning solutions that build digital marketing capability, performance marketing knowledge, and operational readiness within client organizations.

03
Service Line
Corporate Paid Advertising Operations

Enterprise-grade paid media systems covering strategy, full-cycle execution, and real-time optimization across major digital platforms.

04
Service Line
IT and System Infrastructure

Technology infrastructure consulting and deployment for corporate clients building the operational backbone needed for commercial scale.

Market Entry Strategy

The Philippines remains ZelDigital's primary market and the anchor for its 2027 growth push, where demand for digital transformation among small and medium enterprises continues to rise. The company also intends to establish a foothold in the United States and pursue partnerships across global markets — positioning ZelDigital as an internationally operating business growth and technology firm.
